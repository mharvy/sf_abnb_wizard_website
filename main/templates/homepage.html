The Price Estimation Tool shows the expected weekly price of an AirBnB based on location coordinates alone. It is useful for finding out which areas are more pricey than others, or for finding a good weekly price to start an AirBnB at. It uses a distance weighted algorithm, along with Haversine's formula to get extremely accurate results!

The Bookings Optimization Tool shows what daily price one can sell at on any given day to make sure the AirBnB gets booked, while still maximizing profit. It is useful for current AirBnBs that aren't producing enough profit due to lack of bookings. It uses a distance weighted algorithm to look at other close ABnBs, and then find what prices they are selling at, so you can sell slightly lower.

The Investment Strategy Tool gives a good estimation on where to best invest money in a San Fransisco AirBnB. It finds the average price per airbnb (per bedroom!) for every neighborhood, and finds which neighborhood will produce the highest returns

Informatics shows relevant data about the modern AirBnB market. From the neighborhood rating system to which neighborhoods have the highest potential returns, the informatics section has everything you need to own a successful AirBnB
